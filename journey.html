Journeying Through Learning: From Choices to Achievements

Here on this page, my story unfolds a tale of decisions, aspirations, and growth. Let's embark on this journey together.

As the horizon of college approached, I faced the formidable task of choosing a course that would shape my future. Read more

In those high school days, I believed that a popular engineering course would guarantee success. With my math skills not far from impressive, I dabbled in drafting, eager to lay my foundation.

But the pandemic swept in, shattering my illusions. It became evident that a course alone couldn't carve a path to success; it was about how I shaped it. A humbling realization led me to conclude that engineering wasn't my calling. A search for my true passion commenced.

Enter Don Honorio Ventura State University (DHVSU), my beacon of hope. From a myriad of courses, two caught my eye Computer Science and Information Technology. After careful contemplation, I chose Information Technology, influenced by DHVSU's extension campus near my home.

Anticipating the journey ahead, I immersed myself in self-study, tackling the enigmatic realm of programming. From Hello World to more complex codes, I treaded this path before college, arming myself with a semblance of knowledge.

College days arrived, and with them, the realization that knowing the terms wasn't enough. Solving problems independently presented challenges. My mind knew conditionals, yet struggled to apply them. This marked the genesis of true progress.

Mistakes turned into stepping stones as I delved into programming logic. YouTube tutorials taught me how to think like a programmer, and a beacon of enlightenment appeared CS50. Through CS50's Introduction to Programming with Python, I revisited the basics, appreciating the nuances of correct coding.

My academic journey intertwined with the CS50 course, expanding my prowess. But, as an honors student, time became a rarity. Completion of the CS50 journey demanded a final project my creation inspired by ChatGpt. A chatbot, a manual response system driven by input probabilities, emerged.

CS50X beckoned, and I ventured into HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Lectures imparted knowledge, but before solving problems, I plunged into the Odin Project, crafting projects like rock-paper-scissors and a calculator, fostering independent creativity.

Beyond programming, college life thrived. Despite the challenges, being an honor student and aspiring programmer, I managed to excel. My first-year accolades include President Lister and Most Outstanding Student in my department.

As the second year rolled in, this webpage emerged, encapsulating my growth. It's more than just a programming journey; it's a testament to my pursuit of personal success. My dreams aren't extravagant; they involve the freedom to reward my parents and myself. This webpage stands as a reminder a virtual pat on the back, urging me forward.

So here we are, traversing the chapters of my story, from course choices to academic achievements, and beyond. The journey is far from over, but looking back, I've come a long way, and that's something to be proud of.
